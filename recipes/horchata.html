Horchata De Arroz

Description

Horchata de arroz is a sweet and refreshing drink and is made from rice soaked in water.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked long grain white rice
  • 3 1/2 cups cold water
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place the rice in a bowl with enough water to cover it and let it soak over night.
  2. Strain the rice and discard the water. Stir the cold water and evaporated milk together. Place the drained rice into a blender along with half of the diluted milk. Blend until rice is finely ground, about 30 seconds. Add the sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla; bland well. Pour in the remaining diluted milk; blend
  3. Line a strainer with two layers of cheesecloth. Place the strainer over another bowl to catch the liquid. Strain the rice milk through the cheesecloth, discard the solids. Repeat the process if necessary. Serve over ice.
