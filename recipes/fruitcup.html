Mexican Fruit Cup

Description

You might think, what is so special about fruit cups? It's just as the name says, fruit in a cup. To Mexicans however, this is great delicious snack and it is because of how they made it into their own snack with all of the ingredients they add. Ingredients such as Tajin and chamoy that make this snack sweet, salty, sour, and spicy.

Steps

  1. In a cup add the fruits of your choosing
  2. Top with a sprinkle of Tajin, a drizzle of chamoy, and a squeeze of lime.
  3. Enjoy!
