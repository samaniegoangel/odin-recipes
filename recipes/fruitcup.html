Mexican Fruit Cup

Description

You might think whats so special about fruit cups, It's just as the name saya, fruit in a cup. To Mexicans however this is great delicious snak and its because of how they made it into their on snack with all of the ingredients they add. Ingredients such as Tajin and chamoy the make this snack sweet, salty, sour, and spicy.

Steps

  1. In a cup add the fruits of your choosing
  2. Top with a sprinkle of Tajin, a drizzle of chamoy, and a squeeze of lime.
  3. Enjoy!
